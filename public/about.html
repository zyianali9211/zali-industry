ZALI Industries is a custom sublimated apparel manufacturer based in Sialkot, Pakistan. We make performance, casual, and surf apparel for brands across the US, UK, EU, Australia, and the Middle East. We're a small operation by industry standards. That's the point.

The Founder

Why I started ZALI.

Founder SIA · 32.49°N
Built by hand,
by people I know.Founder Portrait · Lookbook Vol. 04

I started ZALI because I wanted to build a manufacturing setup where communication is direct and the customer actually knows who is handling their order.

In this industry, a lot of brands deal with agents, traders, or people who pass the order from one place to another. That usually creates delays, confusion, and mistakes. I wanted ZALI to be different.

We are based in Sialkot, Pakistan, and today we are a team of around 30 people working across design, cutting, printing, stitching, packing, and quality control.

I personally stay involved in client communication, design approvals, production updates, and final checking. That is important to me because custom apparel is not just about making the garment. It is about understanding the brand, getting the details right, and keeping the customer updated throughout the process.

We are not trying to act like the biggest factory in the city. Our focus is on being reliable, easy to communicate with, and flexible enough to work with brands that need custom products made properly.

When you work with ZALI, you are not dealing with a random middleman. You are working directly with the team that is managing your order from start to finish.

Zyian Ali
Founder · ZALI Industries
Why Sialkot

The world's capital of Fitnesswear.

Most of the world's match-grade soccer balls, Olympic-spec hockey gear, and pro fightwear comes from Sialkot. The city has been making sporting goods for over a century, since the British Raj era. Three generations of stitchers, cutters, and printers have grown up in this trade.

When you order from ZALI, you're not just getting a factory. You're getting access to an entire ecosystem of suppliers, fabric mills, trim makers, and specialty artisans, all within a 15-minute drive from our facility.
