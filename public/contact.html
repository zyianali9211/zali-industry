Shipping Terms

We ship globally.

We support multiple international shipping terms, DDU, DDP, CNF, and FOB, so brands, suppliers, and distributors can manage imports in whatever way works best for them. Default is DDP (duties prepaid, door-to-door) via DHL, FedEx, or UPS; sea freight available for orders over 1,000 pieces.
