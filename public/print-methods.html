No outsourcing. No hand-offs to a third-party printer that never picks up the phone. Every print method below happens in our Sialkot facility, with our own equipment, run by people who've been printing for 10+ years.

METHOD 01 MOST POPULAR

Dye Sublimation

Heat-pressed at 200°C, sublimation ink turns into gas and bonds with polyester at the molecular level. The result: full-coverage prints that will not crack, fade, or peel for the life of the garment.

Best For
All-Over Prints

CMYK Full

  • Zero hand-feel, the print becomes part of the fabric
  • Vivid color saturation, photo-realistic gradients
  • Wash-tested to 50+ cycles with no fade
  • Only works on white or light polyester base fabric

METHOD 02

Screen Print

The classic. Plastisol and water-based inks pushed through hand-prepped screens. Built for cotton tees, hoodies, and bold graphic work where opacity and tactile feel matter.

Best For
Cotton Tees
Fabric

Colors

  • Soft-hand discharge available for premium fashion finish
  • Plastisol or water-based, eco-certified inks on request
  • Set-up cost amortizes well at higher quantities
  • Best for solid graphics, logos, and limited color counts

METHOD 03

DTF Print

Direct-to-film. The newest, most flexible print method in our shop. Multicolor designs printed on transfer film and heat-pressed onto any fabric. No screen setup, no MOQ on the print itself.

Best For
Short Runs
Fabric
Anything
Min Order
25 pcs
Colors
Unlimited
  • Photo-realistic detail, gradients, fine line work
  • No set-up fee, no screen costs, no minimums per design
  • Works on cotton, poly blends, fleece, hoodies, and caps
  • Best fit for short runs and SKU variations within one order
  • Slight hand-feel, less ideal for full-coverage all-over print
